# Senior Backend Engineer — Contoso Analytics

Contoso Analytics builds a real-time analytics platform for logistics companies. We are
hiring a Senior Backend Engineer to own our event ingestion and processing systems.

## What you'll do

- Design and operate high-throughput, event-driven data pipelines (Kafka or similar)
- Lead our ongoing migration to Kubernetes and infrastructure-as-code
- Raise the bar on observability, on-call health, and incident response
- Mentor mid-level engineers and drive alignment through design documents

## What we're looking for

- 5+ years building backend services in Go, TypeScript, or a similar language
- Production experience with Kubernetes, Terraform, and a major cloud provider (we use AWS)
- Strong PostgreSQL skills; experience with streaming systems (Kafka) a big plus
- A track record of mentoring and clear technical writing
- Nice to have: experience with usage-based billing systems

## What we offer

Remote-first (Canada), meaningful ownership, and a platform processing billions of events.
